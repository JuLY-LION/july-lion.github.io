The SH Avalon Primer

So, what exactly is SH Avalon and what's it about?

SH Avalon is a new game mode inspired by Avalon: The Resistance for secrethitler.io that introduces 3 different roles to the game of Secret Hitler.

  1. Merlin - A liberal role that knows the entire fascist team. The fascists all appear red on his screen (however, he doesn't know who Hitler is).
  2. Morgana - A regular fascist who will appear as a Merlin candidate to Percival (will expand on in the next section). It is important to note that Hitler can never be Morgana. To clarify, the fascist who is Morgana will know that they are Morgana.
  3. Percival - A liberal role that sees two names on his screen with different colors than the other players. These players contain both Merlin and Morgana.

Cool, so if I'm Merlin, what's stopping me from just claiming all my information from the get go?

Because! Fascists now have a new win condition. If liberals "win the game," either via executing Hitler, or by getting 5 liberal policies on the board, at the end of the game Hitler gets to select one person to assassinate (the fascists get revealed to Hitler during assasination). If they assassinate Merlin, then fascists still win. So be careful as Merlin to not give out too much info!

Important Clarification: Merlin getting shot only results in a Fascist win if he is shot during the post-game assasination, not if he is shot via the bullet that results as a result of the 4th and 5th fascist policy being enacted. If he is shot as a result of the latter, the game continues on as normal, and no mention is made whether the deceased player was Merlin or not.
